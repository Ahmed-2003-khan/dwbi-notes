1. ROLAP (Relational OLAP) Kya Hai?

Definition: ROLAP ka matlab hai Relational Online Analytical Processing. Is approach mein OLAP data ko Relational Databases (RDBMS) mein store kiya jata hai.

ROLAP Kyun Use Karte Hain?

MOLAP ko ek badi problem hoti hai ‚Äî Curse of Dimensionality. Jab data bohot zyada ho jaye to MOLAP slow ho jata hai.

  • Scalability: ROLAP bohot large data handle kar sakta hai.
  • Aggregate Awareness: Pre-built summary tables ka use karke fast querying hoti hai.

ROLAP as a "Cube"

ROLAP mein cube physical form mein nahi hota ‚Äî Fact Table ko ek "unrolled cube" samajhna chahiye.

Data rows/columns ki form mein hota hai, lekin logically multidimensional analysis ho raha hota hai.

2. ROLAP mein Cube Kaise Banta Hai? (SQL Implementation)

Agar aap Product √ó Month √ó Sales analyze karna chahte hain to normal SQL mein multiple queries likhni padti hain.

  • Total sales per Product & Month
  • Row totals
  • Column totals

Problem: Dimensions barhne par queries exponential ho jati hain ‚Üí bohot slow.

Solution: GROUP BY CUBE Clause

Fayda: Saari combinations ek hi query mein generate ho jati hain.

3. ROLAP Issues (Exam Important)

  1. Maintenance Issue: Summary tables ko frequently update karna parta hai.
  2. Storage Explosion: Har cheez aggregate karne se size hadd se zyada barh jata hai.
  3. Non-Standard Hierarchies: Dimensions hamesha linear nahi hote.
    Example: ‚ÄúGreen items‚Äù ‚Üí fridge bhi ho sakta, washing machine bhi.
  4. Conventions Issue: Calendar vs Fiscal, Week start (Sunday ya Monday?) ‚Äî ROLAP design ko confuse karta hai.

4. Other OLAP Architectures

HOLAP (Hybrid OLAP)

  • MOLAP + ROLAP ka hybrid.
  • Detailed data RDBMS mein, aggregates MOLAP cube mein.

DOLAP (Desktop OLAP)

  • Cube ka ek chhota hissa user ke laptop par download ho jata hai.
  • Local/offline analysis possible hota hai.

5. Dimensional Modeling (DM) vs ER Modeling

ER Modeling (Old OLTP Systems)

  • Bohot complex ‚ÄúSpider web structure‚Äù.
  • Reporting ke liye joins bohot slow ‚Üí O(n¬≤).
  • Data store easy, analyze difficult.

Dimensional Modeling (DM)

  • DSS/Decision Making ke liye design.
  • Center mein Fact Table, surrounding mein Dimension Tables.
  • Fact table ‚Üí multi-part key, Dimensions ‚Üí single key.

ER vs DM (Comparison)

Feature ER Modeling Dimensional Modeling
Purpose Transaction optimize Analysis optimize
Structure Complex & micro-relationships Simple & macro-relationships
Visibility Spider-web like Star-like, easy
Joins Too many joins (slow) Few joins (fast)

6. Star Schema (Core of DM)

Structure

  1. Fact Table: Numeric measures (Sales, Quantity) + foreign keys.
  2. Dimension Tables: Descriptive attributes (Product, Date, Store).

Benefits

  • Simple ‚Üí Business users easily understand.
  • Fast queries due to fewer joins.
  • De-normalization for performance.
